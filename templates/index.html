What is this?

Clinical Intelligence on Demand

This platform is designed to detect lung cancer from histological images by leveraging advanced deep learning models. The system is fully automated, providing high-precision diagnostic support, and also offers explainability features to help clinicians understand and trust the underlying predictions.

Why Automation

Faster, insightful, scalable, dynamic.

Our comprehensive system is able to distinguish lung cancer (adecarcinoma, large cell carcinoma, squamous cell carcinoma) and normal histological lung images.
